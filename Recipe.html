Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.
  3. Gradually add the flour and baking soda.
  4. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. Drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until golden brown.
